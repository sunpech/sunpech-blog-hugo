Greekfest LA

Posted on September 11, 2012 by Steven Suwatanapongched
 Los Angeles

One of the things I love about living in Los Angeles is all the ethnic fairs that go on. Over the weekend I attended two different fairs no so far from where I live.

On Friday there was LA Greekfest. I went during the evening to avoid the crowd and heat during the day. My favorite dish was the dessert, the pastry puffs.
