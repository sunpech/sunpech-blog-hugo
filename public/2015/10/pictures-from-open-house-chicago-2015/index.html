Pictures from Open House Chicago 2015

Posted on October 19, 2015 by Steven Suwatanapongched
 Chicago,  Photography,  Photowalk

On Sunday I went to checkout Open House Chicago and did a little photowalk of my own. I actually had no plan to go until my sister gave me her priority pass the morning of. But I’m glad I did as it was not only had some good photops, but was a nice steps in too since I use FitBit.

The places I visited:

  • The Oriental Theatre
  • Joffrey Ballet
  • Aon Center
  • Continental Illinois Bank Building
  • The Rookery

My favorite spot was the Aon Center. I went up to the 71st floor and had a 360 degree view of Chicago from the third tallest building in the city.
