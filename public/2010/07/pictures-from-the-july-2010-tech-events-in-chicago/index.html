Pictures from the July 2010 Tech Events in Chicago

Posted on July 20, 2010 by Steven Suwatanapongched

I made sure to take a lot of photos this month.

Tuesday July 6, 2010

Chicago Ruby had a meeting over at ThoughtWorks. Snapped a few photos there. Very cool talks. And of course, pizza. You can sign up for these meetings at: Chicago Ruby Meetup. There was a Hack Night on Thursday of the week, but I did not take any photos.

Presentations by @chrisjpowers and @blakesmith.

Wednesday July 14, 2010

Here are the pictures from the MongoDB 101 presentation by JC Grubbs (@thegrubbsian). It was quite a large group that attended. Also the first time that I snapped up photos with my Canon Rebel XT.
