Pictures from SF Ruby December Meetup

Posted on December 9, 2011 by Steven Suwatanapongched
 San Francisco,  Software Development,  Meetup

I drove up to San Francisco from LA to check out the SF Ruby group. The drive was long, but worth it as I got to meet some interesting people. The meetup was hosted at ZenDesk. Being from Chicago and now living in LA, I never before heard so much talk about venture capital (VCs) coming from developers. I think there really is a culture difference in SF compared to other cities. Just an observation.
