Reading List for Summer 2011

Posted on August 3, 2011 by Steven Suwatanapongched
 Books

I have selected the books I’d like to complete before the end of Summer/Fall season. I think it’s a nice set and variety of books.

  • Confessions of a Public Speaker by Scott Berkun
  • Peopleware: Productive Projects and Teams by Tom DeMarco and Timothy Lister
  • Eloquent Ruby by Russ Olsen
  • Four Steps to Epiphany by Steven Gary Blank
  • The Camera by Ansel Adams
  • Joel on Software by Joel Spolsky
