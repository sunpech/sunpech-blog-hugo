Food Is Happiness

Posted on March 1, 2011 by Steven Suwatanapongched
 Personal

I started a new blog/website over at foodishappiness.com. I exported all my food posts from this site as well as from bestburgerintown.blogspot.com (which I’ve abandoned) and imported them into the new site. It’s just something new I wanted to try out, to see if I could generate content from my food/eating passion. I’m still on an “eating tour” of Bangkok, Thailand so to speak.

I know many say that blogs are dead. I certainly hope not. As much as I like Twitter, I just don’t think 140 characters is enough to express many things.

So read up on my food adventures, please turn to Food Is Happiness site or follow @foodishappiness on the Twitter. This site is still my personal blog, which will be less on food, more on photography, travel, and maybe some books. Happy eating!
