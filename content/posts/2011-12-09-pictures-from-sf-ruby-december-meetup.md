I drove up to San Francisco from LA to check out the [SF Ruby group](https://www.sfruby.info). The drive was long, but worth it as I got to meet some interesting people. The meetup was hosted at [ZenDesk](https://www.zendesk.com). Being from Chicago and now living in LA, I never before heard so much talk about venture capital (VCs) coming from developers. I think there really is a culture difference in SF compared to other cities. Just an observation.
